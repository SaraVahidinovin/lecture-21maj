Apple Pie Cake Recipe

ingredients:

Topping

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter

Appli Mixture

  • 6 cups thinly sliced peeled apples (6 medium)
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice

Cake

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/3 cups water
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 3 eggs

Instruction:

  1. Heat oven to 350°F (325°F for dark or nonstick pan). Spray bottom and sides of 13x9-inch pan cooking spray.
  2. In medium bowl, stir together flour, 1/2 cup brown sugar and the salt. With pastry blender or fork, cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, gently stir together Apple Mixture ingredients; set aside.
  4. In large bowl, beat Cake ingredients with electric mixer on medium speed 2 minutes, scraping bowl occasionally. Pour into pan.
  5. Arrange apple mixture evenly over cake batter; press lightly into batter. Sprinkle with Topping.
  6. Bake 40 to 45 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely, about 2 hours. Store covered at room temperature.
